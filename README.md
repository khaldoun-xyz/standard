## Gild

Gild is our learning community to help data scientists do great work.

### Why?

Technology develops like an onion.
The newest layers develop on top of earlier ones.

<img src="imgs/sketch-concentric_circles.png" width="200">

If you want to do great work, you need to continuously add new layers.
And that requires being in the details at every stage of the journey.

[YouTube video: In the details](https://youtu.be/-JnqbfeaXXA?si=QPGZGooSF8NOWQrL&t=200)

### But AI will take care of the basics for me

In early 2026, the highest-paid software employees are those that
know how to develop AI. They know the basics and are in the details.
