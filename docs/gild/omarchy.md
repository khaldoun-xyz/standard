## Typical Omarchy commands

### Navigating Omarchy menus

- The command of all commands: SUPER + k
- Launch apps: SUPER + SPACE
- Omarchy menu (for system updates, ...): SUPER + ALT + SPACE

### Navigating Omarchy

- Open Terminal: SUPER + ENTER
- Make a window in your workspace full screen: SUPER ALT + f
- Open the scratchpad: SUPER + s
- Open a browser: SUPER SHIFT + b
- Open Wifi settings: SUPER CONTROL + w
  - when you connect to a public wifi, you need to type "neverssl.com"
    in a browser to force the pop up open.
- Open bluetooth settings: SUPER CONTROL + b
- Close tabs & windows: CONTROL + w (terminals are closed with CONTROL + d,
  TUI interfaces with q)
- Copy/paste: SUPER c/v (alternative for pasting in the terminal: CTRL SHIFT + v)
